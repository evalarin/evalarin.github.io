HTML, Where it Started and Where it is Going

Future HTML

Looking Ahead

The world of technology is expanding and growing daily, and more people are using the internet to browse, create, and interact. HTML continues to evolve to meet modern needs such as improved connectivity, more storage, offline capabilities, integration with applications, stronger performance, and better accessibility.

Future HTML Focus Areas
  • More powerful multimedia and animation without plugins.
  • Better tools for accessibility & stronger semantic structure.
  • Smarter responsive layouts for all devices.
  • Deeper integration with APIs, AI, and web applications.
